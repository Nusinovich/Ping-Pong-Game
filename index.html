Let's Play some PingPong

Game Rules

  • The arrow keys on your keyboard will be used to control the paddle on the left.
  • The player who fails to hit the ball back, loses the point.
  • The game is played until one player reaches 5 points.
  • The game difficulty will increase with each level you reach.
  • To be champion, you will need to win all 5 levels.
  • Remember to have fun!!!
